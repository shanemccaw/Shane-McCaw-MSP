## Brand & Style
The design system is engineered to project expertise, organization, and unwavering reliability for high-level consultancy. The brand personality is "The Trusted Advisor"—calm under pressure, meticulously organized, and fundamentally efficient. 

The aesthetic follows a **Modern Corporate Minimalism** approach. It prioritizes clarity and functional density over decorative flair. The interface uses a card-based architecture to modularize complex data, utilizing generous whitespace to prevent cognitive overload. Every element is aligned to a strict mathematical grid to evoke a sense of structural integrity and professional discipline.

## Colors
The palette is anchored by "Deep Slate" (Primary), providing a grounded and authoritative foundation. "Cool Gray" (Secondary) is reserved for metadata and de-emphasized text to maintain hierarchy. 

"Corporate Teal" serves as the primary action color, used sparingly for CTA buttons, progress indicators, and active states to ensure high visibility against the neutral backdrop. Surfaces utilize a range of very light grays and crisp whites to create distinct containment areas without the "heaviness" of dark borders.

## Typography
This design system utilizes **Inter** for all UI roles to leverage its exceptional legibility in data-heavy environments. The typographic scale is highly disciplined, using weight (SemiBold/700) and color (Primary vs. Secondary) rather than drastic size changes to establish hierarchy. 

Label styles are set in uppercase with slight letter spacing to differentiate them from body copy at a glance. For technical or ID-based data strings, a secondary monospace font is used to ensure character distinction.

## Layout & Spacing
The layout relies on a **Fixed-Fluid Hybrid Grid**. Content is centered within a 1280px container on desktop, while internal components utilize an 8px soft-grid system for all padding and margins.

- **Desktop:** 12-column grid with 24px gutters.
- **Tablet:** 8-column grid with 20px gutters.
- **Mobile:** 4-column grid with 16px gutters and 16px side margins.

Spacing should be generous between major sections (64px+) but tight within functional groups (8px-16px) to reinforce the "organized" brand personality.

## Elevation & Depth
Elevation is communicated through **Tonal Layering** and **Ambient Shadows**. Instead of heavy borders, the design system uses subtle background shifts (White on Light Gray) to denote hierarchy.

- **Level 0 (Background):** #F8FAFC.
- **Level 1 (Cards/Sidebar):** White (#FFFFFF) with a very soft, diffused shadow: `0 2px 10px rgba(15, 23, 42, 0.04)`.
- **Level 2 (Hover/Active):** Slightly more pronounced shadow: `0 10px 25px rgba(15, 23, 42, 0.08)`.
- **Level 3 (Modals):** High-diffusion shadow: `0 20px 48px rgba(15, 23, 42, 0.12)`.

## Shapes
The shape language is **Soft (0.25rem base)**. This provides a modern, approachable feel while maintaining the professional rigor of straight lines. 

- **Standard Elements (Buttons, Inputs):** 4px (0.25rem).
- **Cards & Large Containers:** 8px (0.5rem).
- **Progress Bars & Badges:** Full pill-round (999px) to contrast against the structured grid of the layout.

## Components

### Kanban Boards
Columns should have a transparent background with a dashed 1px border. Task cards use the "Level 1" elevation style. Ensure a clear vertical drag-and-drop indicator using the Teal accent color.

### Status Badges
Small, low-contrast pills. For example, a "Completed" badge uses a light teal background with dark teal text. Do not use high-saturation red/green unless for critical errors/successes; stick to muted corporate tones.

### Progress Bars
Ultra-thin (4px height) tracks using a light gray background. The fill uses a Teal gradient. For high-priority projects, the track height may increase to 8px with a percentage label positioned above it.

### Service Cards
A structured header with a thin-stroke icon (24px) followed by a 3-line truncated description. The bottom of the card should contain a "metadata row" with small-caps labels for "Status," "Deadline," or "Owner."

### Input Fields
Strictly rectangular (4px radius) with a 1px border (#E2E8F0). On focus, the border changes to Primary Blue with a 2px outer glow in light teal. Icons inside inputs should be 20px with a 1.5px stroke weight.
